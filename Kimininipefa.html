Mission

We exist to build and expand the kingdom of God by preaching and teaching the holistic Gospel of Jesus Christ for the transformation and fulfillment of life (Matthew 28:18-20).

Vision

To be a church that knows Christ and makes him known in its area of jurisdiction and to the whole world.

History

PEFA Church Kiminini is one of the Local Church Assembles of PEFA in Kitale West District, Western North Rift Region of the PEFA Church. The church has a rich history dating back in the mid 1960's since it started as a local church Assembly. It is a fairly large church of about 500 congregants with a wide range of diversities and spiritual needs which is addressed through Sunday, Wednesday and Friday services in the the eleven blocks. It has distinct ministries i.e. men, women (UWP), Sunday school and Youth.

This location exposes the church to intervention with political, economic and social cultural issues both locally and national levels.

This is attested by its diverse stakeholder type of numbers and the strategic engagement it assumes across PEFA fraternity.

The women (UWP) meets on Wednesday in the above mentioned blocks under their leadership. The church has a praise and worship team church welfare, men’s welfare and men’s fellowship that congregates ounce per month.

There are ministries for men, women (UWP), Sunday school, and youth. The women’s group meets on Wednesdays, and the men’s fellowship meets monthly. The church also has a vibrant praise and worship team, and both men’s and general church welfare ministries.

Core Beliefs and Values

  1. We value the Holy Bible as the inerrant, inspired Word of God.
  2. We value God’s unconditional love through Christ's redemptive work on the cross.
  3. We value prayer, believer’s baptism, stewardship, and spiritual gifts.
  4. We value our royal priesthood calling to evangelize and disciple.
  5. We value worship through prayer, praise, and teaching.
  6. We value unity in the Body of Christ and compassionate fellowship.
  7. We value honesty, integrity, and accountability in all walks of life.
  8. We value cooperation with other churches in evangelism and missions.
  9. We value discipleship for all ages and democratic church governance.
